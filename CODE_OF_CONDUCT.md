# Code of Conduct

## Standard

We follow the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/). The short version:

- **Be respectful.** Disagree on the work, never on the person.
- **Be patient.** Not everyone speaks English natively or has the same experience level.
- **Be honest.** Don't oversell, don't blame, don't demand.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, slurs, or sustained personal attacks
- Doxing or sharing private information without consent
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or vendor advocacy disguised as contribution
- Anything illegal in the jurisdiction of either party

## Enforcement

The repository owner ([@Achitokun14](https://github.com/Achitokun14)) is the sole arbiter. Reports go to **ali@jets.partners**. Possible actions:

| First incident | Repeat |
|---|---|
| Warning | Temporary ban |
| Comment removed | Permanent ban from issues / PRs / discussions |

The owner reserves the right to remove comments, close issues, and block accounts at their discretion. There is no appeal process.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces: issues, PRs, discussions, commit messages, the wiki (if any), and any public communication that references the project.
